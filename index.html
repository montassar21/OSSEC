About Us

OSSEC

Open Source Software Ensi Club was founded by young and motivated engineering students of the National School of Computer Science (ENSI) back in 2011. Our main mission has been, for the past 10 years , to diffuse the philosophy of open source among Tunisian students by the means of our many events , especially our TuniHhack 's 7th edition .
